About Sohoj Biniyog

Sohoj Biniyog is a transparent and Shariah‑compliant investment platform that connects investors with small and medium‑sized businesses (SMEs) in Bangladesh. Our mission is to empower local entrepreneurs and provide investors with safe, ethical, and rewarding opportunities.

We believe in simple access to investments, fair returns, and fostering economic growth through community support. With our digital platform, investors can diversify portfolios, track performance, and contribute to sustainable development.

Our Values

  • Transparency and trust
  • Shariah compliance
  • Supporting local SMEs
  • Financial inclusion
